How it works

Three simple steps to never do it dolo again.

🗓️
STEP 1

Create an activity

Pick what you're doing, when, and where. Coffee at 9, a workout, a beach day — it takes seconds.

🔗
STEP 2

Share with friends

Send it out so friends can see what you're up to and ask to join in with a tap.

🤝
STEP 3

Tag along together

Approve who's coming, chat with the group, and meet up in real life.

Everything you need to make plans real

Your Schedule
